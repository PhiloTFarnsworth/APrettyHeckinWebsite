Why Another Heckin' Website?

In deference to the great luminaries of the past, we should expect more from websites that purport to be the best {expletive} website. A Pretty Heckin' website meekly submits that beyond making your site small, maybe you could think about accessibility and if it's not too much a bother maybe don't just use a more than like, 5 bullet points in a row.

Section Headings Aren't For Emphasis: A Modest Proposal

I don't want to disagree with such strongly worded convictions of our sainted forefathers, but when writing a humble static site using headers in a way that clues the reader in on what's about to be discussed. Not only will using relevant headers help your readers follow the content of your page, but it might stop you from I don't know, using 14 bullet points in a row on some tangentially related topics. Other Informational Widgets

It's the year 2022, so we can also start to enjoy some newer widgets, like details and meter. If you don't think a comment merits an aside, details1 makes a convenient footnote tag while meter is a built-in progress bar. These are definitely more contextual for usage, but using both might be better than an additional 10 mB in your node-modules folder.
