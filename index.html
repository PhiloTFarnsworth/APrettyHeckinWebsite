Why Another Heckin' Website?

In deference to the great luminaries of the past, we should expect more from websites that purport to be the best {expletive} website. A Pretty Heckin' website meekly submits that beyond making your site small, maybe you could think about accessibility and if it's not too much a bother maybe don't just use a more than like, 5 bullet points in a row.

Section Headings Aren't For Emphasis: A Modest Proposal

I don't want to disagree with such strongly worded convictions of our sainted forefathers, but when writing a humble static site using headers in a way that clues the reader in on what's about to be discussed. Not only will using relevant headers help your readers follow the content of your page, but it might stop you from I don't know, using 14 bulletpoints in a row on some tangetically related topics. Fun Tip

An aside can be better place to deliver rebukes to your reader's intelligence. The goal of your website is to prove how intelligent you are, not how slovenly and homely the reader reading this is.
